For All of Your Golfing Needs!

Hello! We are SOJO Golf! A golfing business based out of South Jordan, Utah. We have all of your golfing needs in mind. Here, our mission is to provide you a one stop trip for all of your Golfing needs. Either stop by the store, or schedule an appointment to meet with one of our Licensed Golf Experts.

Founded in 2020, SOJO Golf strives to be the one-stop place for all of your golfing needs. Locally Owned and Locally Employed! We are always striving to be the best in the business. Those needs include the following:



  • Golfing Lessons
  • Driving Range
  • Custom Club Store
  • Flexible Tee Times
  • Challenging 18-hole course
  • Full-service Bar
  • Discount Golf Wednesdays
  • Senior Golf Discount
  • Student Passes

    • About our Founder

    Founded by Collin Cramer in 2020. Collin Cramer has always had a love for the game of golf. The idea for SOJO golf came from Collin visiting many golf courses, and they were either overpriced or missing some sort of feature that Collin deemed were necessary. It was decided that day that Collin Cramer would open the best Golfing center in all of Utah. Top Golf? Nah. Mulligans? Too old. This new course is the way of the future!
